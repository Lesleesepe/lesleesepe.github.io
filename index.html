Though I have been crocheting for over 20 years, I began creating amigurumi (crocheted stuffed animals) as a project for my son in 2018. I quickly fell in love with making these cute little creatures and saw that many were interested in purchasing my items! I have always been very interested in all types of art, and so have dabbled in many! These include: crochet, knitting, embroidery, clay, painting, dyeing, and more!

Pricing on all items depends on many things: complexity of the pattern; time involved; type of yarn; cost of yarn; types of attachments, etc. If you are interested in a particular item, feel free to message me for a pricing quote. Any quote given will not include shipping cost unless explicitly stated.

All items are made to order unless otherwise indicated. Items that I post that are in stock and ready for purchase will not be customizable unless otherwise noted.

A non-refundable payment/deposit of 50% is required before I will begin a project. Payments can be broken up into several installments if desired, however I will not begin on the project until I have received 50% of the quoted price and I will not ship the product until full payment (plus shipping cost, unless otherwise explicitely stated) is received. Full payment of the remaining balance and shipping is due upon completion (when final photos have been sent) unless a payment plan is explicitly agreed upon BEFORE an initial deposit is made. Payment is accepted by cash, Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, Facebook Pay, or Cashapp. Please be aware that PayPal Purchase Protection (which comes with using the Goods and Services function) does not apply to custom made goods under their policies. It only applies to failure to deliver. See PayPal policy regarding custom goods here: https://www.paypal.com/.../mpp/paypal-safety-and-security

As of October 2020, USPS has raised their shipping prices for all packages. I will continue to choose the least expensive method of shipping for my customers unless otherwise requested. I will provide a shipping quote with a screenshot before purchasing the shipping label. Shipping outside the US for all items will still be determined by country and weight.

Once deposit is received, the time until your project is completed varies depending on the project itself and where the order is in my project queue! I go as quickly as I can, but complex patterns and stitches do take more time. If you have a certain deadline for your order, please advise when I am giving you a quote. Note that all items are made by hand unless otherwise stated, so my hands can only go fast! If you need a rush placed on your order, there may be a rush charge added. Amount of charge will depend on the item.

I have never encountered an unclaimed item, but just in case! When I finish a custom made item, I will contact the customer via Facebook messenger (or the other original method of contact) with finished photos. If the customer fails to respond in any way within 14 days, items will be considered forfeit and available for sale in my shop in order to recoup lost payment. If the customer responds but does not make payment, I will hold the item only for 30 days (from date of contact with finished photos) before the item is considered forfeit and will be posted in my shop for sale.
